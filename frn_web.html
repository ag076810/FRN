Whitepaper



FRN – Friend Token

FRN aims to be the most widely distributed token on sBCH.

Token Distribution

● Airdrop: 147,000,000,000 FRN (70%) are offered for airdrop event.

● Liquidity: 10,500,000,000 FRN (5%) will be used to provide initial liquidity on MistSwap.

● Developers: 10,500,000,000 FRN (5%) will be as rewards for developers.

● LPs reward: 42,000,000,000 FRN (20%) will be as fund for farming pool to reward liquidity providers.

FRN Use Cases

● FRN can be used to vote for the project direction, such as Ad placements, artist cooperating, disaster donation, etc.

● FRN is used to reward active community members of the Friend community.

● FRN will be used as the governance token of Friend.

Friend (FRN) Roadmap and Vision

Our purpose is to make FRN a community-based, widely distributed and truly DAO project.

Below is the vision of roadmap, you can clearly identify where to make the most impact as a member and join us as a friend.

● Q1 2022 Pre-Phase: Project Initiated

We’ve already announced the airdrop event (inviting) at Dec. 30, 2021. The token amounts of airdrop for one transfer are 10000 FRN. Our Twitter/Telegram/Website will be promoted at this phase.

● Q2-Q4 2022 Phase 1: Expansion

We will start cooperating with people who has influence on social media to push the airdrop progress. The token amounts for airdrop are 70% of total supply, however, we will have a vote event to let FRN holders decide whether we should add liquidity beforehand or not. This will depend on the airdrop progressing, furthermore, token holders can also decide where to use the rest of not distributed airdrop through a vote event.

We will co-work with artists to release unique NFT for funding. 60% of profit will transfer to artists, 15% add to liquidity, 15% buyback and burn tokens, 10% as rewards for developers. The percentage has not been set yet due to the contract with artists is not ready.

● Q1-Q2 2023 Phase 2: Liquidity Added

Liquidity will be added on MistSwap once the airdrop reaches 70% of total supply or community decide to proceed beforehand. In order to encourage liquidity providers, we will airdrop tokens to all liquidity providers every week (20% of total supply).

In addition to release NFT for funding, we will also contact with advertisement providers. Rely on advantages of wildly distributed, advertisements posted on our social media or website should easily be high reaches. More detail will be announced at this phase.

Since we have added liquidity, we will make FRN listed on Marketcap.cash. CoinMarketCap and CoinGecko will also listing if the time permits, otherwise they will postpone to next phase.

● Q2-Q3 2023 Phase 3: Token Buyback

Advertising system will be initiated at this phase. 80% of profit will be used to buyback tokens (burn, add liquidity, rewards for those who click advertisement). 20% of profit will be the rewards for developers. The percentage has not been set yet due to the contract is not ready.

Vote system will also be initiated on website, community can use FRN token to decide project direction more easily including making proposal.

● Q3-Q4 2023 Phase 4: Global Event

If everything goes according to plan, we will start to participate global event such as disaster donation, bidding for First-edition copy of US Constitution, etc. The fund will come from independent NFT selling for every single event.




PS: This whitepaper was prepared without any input from developers who will code the smart contract. Therefore, the timeline may be adjusted, but it should expose to you what we’re building and where we will be.
